FlightFinder

Point your phone at the sky and discover every plane above you.

▶ Get it on Google Play

Key Features

📡

Live Sky Pointer

Hold your phone up and see aircraft labels overlaid on the real sky. Gyroscope + compass + AR camera view for instant identification.

🌍

World Map

Track every flight in your area on a live map. Tap any aircraft to see its route, airline, and destination.

✈️

Flight Details

Rich flight cards with photos, confidence scoring, route visualization, and country tracking — all updated in real time.

🗺️

Country Explorer

Celebrate every time you spot a flight to a new country. Interactive heatmap shows your global coverage.

Privacy First

FlightFinder is designed to be private by default. No accounts, no analytics SDKs, no personal data collection. Your approximate location is sent only to OpenSky Network to fetch nearby aircraft — nothing else leaves your device.

Read the full Privacy Policy →

How It Works

FlightFinder combines public ADS-B data from OpenSky Network and enrichment from ADSBDB to provide comprehensive flight information without requiring any API keys or user accounts. Aircraft positions are refreshed every 20 seconds and smoothly interpolated for fluid real-time tracking.
