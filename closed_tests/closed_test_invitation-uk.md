# Flight Finder: Looking for Beta Testers for Android

I'm reaching out because I've developed an Android app and I need Beta Testers to run a testing campaign before the official release on the Play Store.

**If like me you often watch planes in the sky, dreaming and imagining where they're headed, this app is for you**

# Point your phone at the sky and identify planes

With **FlightFinder**, you can point at planes in the sky and see them displayed in augmented reality, view aircraft models, their airline, and plenty of other data. Flight Finder is completely free.

I need your feedback!!!

The app is in **closed testing** for **14 days** (Google Play requirement). To support me:

1. **Join the Google Group** FlightFinder Beta: [`https://groups.google.com/g/flight-finder-beta`](https://groups.google.com/g/flight-finder-beta)
2. **Download the app** from the Play Store: [https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lewisnclarkexploration.flightfinder](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.lewisnclarkexploration.flightfinder)

No need to actively test every day — just install it and use it whenever you want to identify planes in the sky.
